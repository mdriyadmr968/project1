Stir Fry Green Beans

Stir-fries are fast, easy, and simple, and this classic green bean offering is no different. We go the extra mile here and use 2 slices of bacon for some extra smoky flavor, but you can simply substitute in extra-virgin olive oil if you prefer to go the vegetarian route. If you're vegan, you can also sub out the oyster sauce for more hoisin; just note that your dish will be a bit sweeter than the original version.
